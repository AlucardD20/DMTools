Lovecraftian Oracle Generator

Here are the instructions for how to use the Lovecraftian Oracle Generator:

Enter your question or phrase in the box and select a likelihood then click "Roll for Event" to get an answer. You can generate random names, plot hooks, clues, and more! Random Events will happen on "doubles". Use the buttons above to interact with the oracle and discover strange happenings in your Lovecraftian world. When you are done using the oracle, you can download your entire results in a .txt file for reference. Refreshing, closing/"X'ing" out the page or clicking "Clear Results" will wipe the log.
