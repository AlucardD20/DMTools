Solo Star Wars RPG, Hunting Jedi with an Imperial Inquisitor - E1

April 10th, 2026
In this solo Star Wars RPG session, I try out the ACE system from ENWorld and step into the role of Imperial Inquisitor Cross. The hunt begins on the junkyard moon of Kessler 9, where rumors point to a surviving Jedi hiding in the warehouse district. What starts as a visit to a cantina contact quickly turns into threats, suspicion, and a street chase when somebody takes too much interest in Cross. This is a desktop solo play setup using a map, oracle questions, a custom dice roller, and a simple system that moves fast. If you want to see more of Inquisitor Cross tracking fugitive Jedi, or want a separate look at the ACE system itself, let me know in the comments.
